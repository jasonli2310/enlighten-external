Enlighten Preschool

Bilingual and Outdoor Education

Our Mission

The mission of Enlighten Preschool is to provide young children opportunities to learn and grow in a bilingual, bicultural, natural and developmentally appropriate play-based environment that celebrates the integration of mind, body, and spirit as the cornerstone of success and happiness in our global society




Our Philosophy

Children learn best through play and are able to develop to their full potential in an environment where teachers guide exploration. The outdoor and bilingual curricula are based upon emergent principles where teachers and children research together. We believe children learn by experiencing the curriculum for themselves, so teachers set up interest areas for children to experiment and explore their environment.




Our Campus

Enlighten Preschool campus is a brand new facility built in 2017. There are 8 classrooms throughout the whole facility. Enlighten follows the natural outdoor classroom philosophy in our care and education of young children. The 1.5 acre school yard provides children a broad variety of learning experiences and opportunities to grow in areas such as gross and fine motor development, social-emotional development, language development, and creative expression.
